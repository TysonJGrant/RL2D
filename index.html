HOW TO PLAY (R Key)
Use your car to hit the ball into the opposing players goal

Controls (requires an Xbox controller):
[Right Trigger] Accelerate
[Left Trigger] Reverse
[A] Jump
[A] Dash (after jumping)
[B] Boost
[X] drift
[Left Joystick] Steer
[Left Joystick] Dash in direction (relative to direction car is facing)

[R] Toggle rules
